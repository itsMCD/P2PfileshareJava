As a young girl, Sarah dreamed of exploring the world.
She worked hard and saved up enough money to travel to Paris. 
While there, she met a handsome Frenchman, fell in love, and decided to stay. 
Years later, Sarah was living her dream, running a successful bakery and raising a family with her beloved husband. 
Despite challenges, they remained deeply in love and grateful for the life they had built together. 
Sarah knew that taking a risk had been the best decision she ever made.
